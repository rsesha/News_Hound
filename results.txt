# 2022 FIFA World Cup Winner

**Argentina** won the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

The final match was played at Lusail Stadium in Lusail, Qatar, on December 18, 2022, between Argentina and defending champions France [1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2022_FIFA_World_Cup_Final). Argentina defeated France 3-4 on penalties in a thrilling final, with Lionel Messi scoring twice and lifting the trophy for the first time in his career [3](https://www.sportingnews.com/us/soccer/news/who-won-2022-fifa-world-cup-final-score-result-highlights-qatar/aar5gfviuuapvkmcq3tf7dep). Kylian Mbappe netted a hat-trick for France, but could not prevent their third consecutive World Cup final defeat [3](https://www.sportingnews.com/us/soccer/news/who-won-2022-fifa-world-cup-final-score-result-highlights-qatar/aar5gfviuuapvkmcq3tf7dep).

This was the 22nd edition of FIFA's competition for men's national football teams, with 32 teams competing across 64 matches from November 20 to December 18, 2022 [2](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022).
